The Process

From document to series
in three steps.

No cameras. No studio. No scheduling. Just your document, a headshot, and ten minutes.

01
📄

Upload Your Document

Drop in any PDF — a CBN manual, an onboarding guide, a code of conduct. Claude reads every page and understands the structure, not just the keywords.

02
🧠

Claude Structures and Scripts

Claude identifies key learning objectives and divides content into focused episodes. It writes the script, generates quiz questions with timestamps — all automatically.

03

Your Employees Go Live

YarnGPT clones the voice. D-ID renders the AI avatar from a headshot. The result is a Netflix-style episode that feels like your company made it — because it did.
